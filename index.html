About UMBC Rugby

1984 Founded
NCR Division II
Only Contact Sport at UMBC

Founded in 1984, the UMBC Men's Rugby Club is the only contact sport at UMBC and is a proud member of the National Collegiate Rugby Division II. We are dedicated to excellence in competition and fostering a community of inclusivity and sportsmanship.

Our mission is to develop skilled athletes, supportive teammates, and responsible leaders through rugby. Whether you're new to the sport or experienced, UMBC Rugby offers a welcoming environment to grow and thrive.
