Welcome to UMBC Rugby Alumni

It all started in November 1992 with the playing of the very first UMBC Alumni Game. It was cold that day, it snowed during the game and yes the field slanted uphill both ways.

1992 First Alumni Game
1996 Annual Tradition Begins
1998 Formal Organization
Today Weekend Celebration

Fast forward a decade plus and what started as a Saturday afternoon has become a weekend event that includes honoring our legends, presentation of the Haud Colluni Scholarship, a fantastic post game BBQ, the Best Damn Awards in all of Rugby and the presentation of the Alumni Cup.
