Practices are held on Walker Avenue Field, typically on weekday evenings. Check our schedule for updates.

While regular attendance is encouraged to build skills and teamwork, we understand if you have other commitments. Just communicate with the team captains and coaches.

Our team culture is centered on inclusivity, sportsmanship, and brotherhood. We welcome players of all skill levels and backgrounds to join and contribute to our supportive and competitive environment.

We host several events throughout the year, including the Alumni Weekend, Hall of Fame Ceremony, and post-game socials. These events are open to players, alumni, and fans to celebrate the spirit of UMBC Rugby.
