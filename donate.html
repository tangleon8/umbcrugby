Support UMBC Rugby

We appreciate any and all efforts in supporting UMBC Rugby and its student-athletes. One fun and impactful way to show your support is by cheering us on during our matches! Below are additional ways you can contribute to the growth and success of UMBC Rugby.

Financial Support

As a student-run club, UMBC Rugby receives minimal funding through the Department of Athletics and Recreation. Donations are crucial to our growth and sustainability, providing essential resources for training, scholarships, and travel expenses. Your generosity directly impacts the future of UMBC Rugby.

To donate:

  1. Select "Athletics & Recreation" in the category dropdown.
  2. Choose "Haud Collumi Scholarship Award".

If you prefer, you can click the button below to donate directly:
