Walker Field Location

Our practices and home matches take place at Walker Field on the beautiful UMBC campus. This premier athletic facility serves as the heart of our rugby program and home to countless memorable moments.

Get Directions

Address

Walker Field UMBC Campus 1000 Hilltop Circle, Baltimore, MD 21250

Parking Information

Free parking available in adjacent lots near the athletic facilities. Look for directional signs leading to Walker Field. The stadium parking lot is recommended for easy access.

Public Transportation

UMBC Shuttle System provides convenient campus transportation. Baltimore Public Transit routes 77 and 35 stop directly on campus with easy access to Walker Field.
